ZeBro

A gamified medical learning app that sharpens your eye-diagnosis skills on chest X-rays, ECGs and skin lesions.

Download on the App Store

About

ZeBro is an educational, gamified medical learning application. Swipe through real-world medical images, train your pattern recognition, and learn the visual cues behind common findings.

ZeBro is intended for educational purposes only. The app does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations.
